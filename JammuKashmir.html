Best Places to Visit In Jammu and Kashmir Slider

Srinagar

known as 'Paradise on Earth', famous for the picturesque Dal Lake and its floating houseboats, Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh), and traditional Kashmiri handicrafts.

Gulmarg

A popular hill station and a premier skiing destination, known as the 'Meadow of Flowers'. It boasts the world's second-highest operating cable car, the Gulmarg Gondola.

Pahalgam

A tranquil town nestled on the banks of the Lidder River, famous for its lush green meadows, scenic valleys (like Betaab Valley), and serving as the base camp for the Amarnath Yatra.

Vaishno Devi Temple

A major Hindu pilgrimage destination, located in the Trikuta Mountains, where devotees undertake a trek to the sacred cave shrine of Mata Vaishno Devi.

Sonmarg

Known as the 'Meadow of Gold', this town offers breathtaking views of glaciers and snow-clad mountains, serving as a base for trekking routes to high-altitude lakes.

Best Places to Visit in Ladakh Slider

Pangong Tso Lake

A stunning, high-altitude saltwater lake that changes colour from shades of blue to green and red. A significant portion of the lake lies in China.

Leh Palace

A former royal palace overlooking the town of Leh, built in the 17th century, offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and the Indus Valley.

Nubra Valley

A beautiful valley located north of Leh, famous for its distinct landscape of high-altitude desert, lush greenery, sand dunes, and double-humped Bactrian camels.

Shanti Stupa

A white-domed Buddhist stupa built to promote world peace and prosperity, offering spectacular sunrise and sunset views over Leh and the surrounding mountains.

Hemis Monastery

One of the largest and wealthiest Buddhist monasteries in Ladakh, known for its annual Hemis Festival, which features colourful masked dances.
